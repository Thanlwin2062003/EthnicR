Ethnic Resilience

Empowering ethnic communities in Myanmar

About Us

Ethnic Resilience (EthnicR) is a Myanmar civil society organization (CSO) committed to providing assistance to vulnerable ethnic communities wherever they exist.

Established in 2023, EthnicR was initially a loosely organized collective of volunteers operating in Myanmar's Myawaddy and Kawkayate regions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate and empower ethnic communities, equipping them with the knowledge and resources to actively contribute to a just society.

Education in Emergencies

EthnicR is committed to providing education in emergencies, ensuring equitable and inclusive learning opportunities for individuals of all ages in crisis situations.

Our Vision

EthnicR envisions a future where all ethnic communities in Myanmar, irrespective of their background, have equal access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.
